Natural Textile Dye Plants

We grow many of our own textile dyes on our small urban farm. A few of them are listed below.

  • Black-eyed Susans
  • Calendula
  • Dyer's Coreopsis
  • Elderberries
  • Golden Rod
  • Madder
  • Marigolds
  • Woad

Why We Use Natural Dyes

Natural dyes are beautiful, vibrant, and speak to a sense of place. By using natural dyes, we can source color from plants without the use of synthetic toxic chemicals that harm our water systems and soil. From plant to fiber, we grow, process, and lovingly produce beauitful textiles in our studio space in St. Louis, MO.

Slowing Down

In a world of fast fashion and high levels of consumerism, we aim to create beautiful handmade textile wares that will be treasured for a lifetime. We hope you enjoy your new home wares just as much as we enjoyed creating them for you.
